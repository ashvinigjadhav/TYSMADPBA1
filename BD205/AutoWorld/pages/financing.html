Financing Tips

Check Your Credit Score

A good credit score (750+) can help you get better interest rates. Check your score before applying and work on improving it if needed.

Compare Interest Rates

Shop around with different lenders to find the best rates. Even a 0.5% difference can save thousands over the loan term.

Higher Down Payment

Pay more upfront to reduce your loan amount, monthly EMI, and total interest paid. Aim for at least 20% down payment.

Choose Right Tenure

Shorter loan terms mean higher EMIs but less total interest. Balance your monthly budget with total cost considerations.

Read Terms Carefully

Understand all terms including processing fees, prepayment charges, late payment penalties, and insurance requirements.

Get Pre-Approved

Pre-approval gives you better negotiating power and helps you shop with confidence within your budget range.
