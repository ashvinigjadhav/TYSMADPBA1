About AutoHub

Your trusted partner in premium pre-owned vehicles since 2008

Our Journey

AutoHub began with a simple vision: to revolutionize the pre-owned car buying experience by combining transparency, quality, and exceptional customer service.

Founded in 2008 by automotive enthusiasts, we've grown from a small local dealership to one of the region's most trusted pre-owned car platforms. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has helped thousands of families find their perfect vehicle.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your automotive needs

Vehicle Inspection

Every car undergoes a comprehensive 150-point inspection to ensure quality and reliability. Our certified technicians check everything from engine performance to safety features.

Warranty Coverage

All our vehicles come with comprehensive warranty options, giving you peace of mind and protection for your investment with nationwide coverage.

Financing Solutions

Flexible financing options with competitive rates. Our finance team works with multiple lenders to find the best deal for your budget and credit situation.

Trade-In Program

Get the best value for your current vehicle with our hassle-free trade-in program. Instant online valuations and fair market pricing guaranteed.

Home Delivery

Complete your purchase from home with our contactless delivery service. We bring the car to you with all paperwork handled digitally.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated customer support team is available around the clock to assist with any questions, concerns, or service needs you may have.
